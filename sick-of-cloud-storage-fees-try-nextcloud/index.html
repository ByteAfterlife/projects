Sick of cloud storage fees? Try Nextcloud.

Byte November 29, 2025

If you've been on the internet for a bit of time, you probably heard of cloud storage, I won't say names, but their prices can be very expensive in the long run. Well, there's this thing called Nextcloud, which is run on your systems, meaning you have full control over it, this concept is known as "self-hosting" which means you control your own data and the application is run on your server/computer or multiple if you have a cluster. Anyway Nextcloud does everything most cloud hosts do, it can store your contacts, calendar, files obviously, and people made lots of extensions that let you store way more, like passwords, make forms even, and so on. It also of course supports SSO, so I made sure to integrate Keycloak with it for easy sign in. It also supports WebDAV, which makes alot of modern file managers support transferring files directly from your file manager, I know, awesome!

Have fun with Nextcloud.
